Step 1: Compare the first two numbers.
Step 2: Pick the greatest number out of the two.
Step 3: Take the biggest number and compare it to the next number.
Step 4: Repeat steps 2 to 4 until you reach the end of the list.
Step 5: When you have no more numbers to compare, you have reached the end of the list.
Step 6: The number you finished with is the greatest number of the list.
Step 7: Stop, you have found the biggest number of the list.
